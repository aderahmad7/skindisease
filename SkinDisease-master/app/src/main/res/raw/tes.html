Privacy Policy for Skin Disease

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Skin Disease Detection App. By using our app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1.1 Personal Data

We may collect personal data that you provide voluntarily, such as your email address for support and feedback. This data is used to enhance your experience and facilitate communication.

1.2 Location Data

The app may request access to your location data to provide localized services and improve the accuracy of the analysis. This data will only be used with your consent and will not be stored or shared without your permission.

1.3 Activity Data

We collect activity data related to your interactions with the app, including features accessed and duration of use. This information helps us understand user behavior and improve the app's functionality.

Purpose of Data Collection

The purpose of collecting data is to enable the app to analyze images of skin conditions using AI technology, provide timely and accurate feedback regarding potential skin diseases, and improve the overall user experience.

Data Processing

When you capture images of your skin, these images are processed using advanced AI algorithms to detect potential skin conditions. We also analyze usage patterns to enhance the app's features and address user needs more effectively.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except under specific circumstances. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us in delivering the app's services, ensuring they comply with our privacy standards. Additionally, we may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Use of Data

The data collected is used for several purposes, including analyzing skin conditions and providing feedback, improving app features based on user interactions, and communicating important updates or support information to users.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@skindisease.com.
